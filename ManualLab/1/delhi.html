About DELHI:

Delhi is the capital of INDIA. It is one of the metropolitan cities in INDIA. It is an ancient and advanced city. Indian politics is mainly concentrated in this city. The Supreme Court and defense headquarters are located in the city.

Important places:

1. India Gate
2. Red Fort (Not Taj Mahal, it's in Agra)
3. Ancient Buildings (e.g., Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb)
